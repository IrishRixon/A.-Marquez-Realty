The Lakeshore Medical Center

This exceptional facility brings together top-tier healthcare services with the latest in medical technology, ensuring that residents have access to the best possible care. Living at The Lighthouse Residences means enjoying the convenience of luxurious condo living, with the added assurance of being just steps away from state-of-the-art healthcare facilities. Whether it's routine check-ups or specialized medical treatment, the proximity of The Lakeshore Medical Center offers unparalleled peace of mind. Experience a unique blend of comfort, luxury, and healthcare excellence, all within the vibrant community of Brgy. Panipuan, Mexico, Pampanga.

The Lakeshore

The Lighthouse Residences offers a luxurious living experience strategically located near Lakeshore Pampanga, a tranquil 12-hectare man-made lake. Residents can immerse themselves in a serene environment ideal for various water sports and leisure activities, complemented by lush greenery and a picturesque 2-kilometer jogging trail.

Within this vibrant community, you'll find amenities such as a lakeside restaurant offering exquisite dining with scenic views, the peaceful St. John Paul II Church for reflection and worship, and a stunning lighthouse view deck for enjoying the surrounding landscape. Beautifully landscaped parks provide additional spaces for relaxation and recreation.

The Lighthouse Residences ensures convenience with access to event facilities at Lakeshore Pampanga, perfect for hosting gatherings and celebrations. Experience a lifestyle where every detail is designed to enhance comfort, luxury, and leisure, nestled in the heart of Brgy. Panipuan, Mexico, Pampanga.
