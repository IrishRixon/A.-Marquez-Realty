Montrose
Parkview


Inspired by the European way of life, Montrose Parkview is set to bring the beauty and old-world charms of Europe to Pampanga. From quaint al fresco shops below that offer delectable delights from the sun up till sun down, to wide and green open spaces that let you enjoy outdoor activities that enrich the mind and body, Montrose Parkview is a complete lifestyle experience that makes residents and visitors alike reminisce the charms and beauty of the European culture, everyday.

Shophouse District


Bringing the best of Pampanga. Capital Town’s Shophouse District will be positioned as the main tourist attraction. Taking inspiration from the beautiful cities in Europe, it is envisioned to be urban yet intimate and picturesque.

Combining the Neoclassical Architecture and Art Deco style guidelines, the architecture and design of the Shophouse District will be similar to Rodeo Drive in Los Angeles, California, USA.
